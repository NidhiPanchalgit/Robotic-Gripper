## Robotic Gripper Design
This project involves the design of a robotic gripper mechanism using Autodesk Fusion 360.The gripper is capable of opening and closing to hold objects, 
using a gear-driven linkage system.

Objectives
Design a functional robotic gripping mechanism to enable controlled opening and closing motion and Convert it into rotational motion into gripping action to
ensure stability and precision during operation.

Design Considerations

* Smooth motion transfer between gears and linkages
* Proper alignment of joints
* Grip strength and object stability
* Mechanical efficiency

Working Principle
Rotation of the gear initiates motion, the Gear drives the linkage mechanism so the Linkages transfer motion to gripper arms then Gripper opens and closes
to hold objects securely

Learning Outcomes

* Understanding of gear-driven mechanisms
* Linkage-based motion systems
* CAD assembly and motion design
* Basics of robotic gripping systems
  
Future Improvements

durability testing is also important.
